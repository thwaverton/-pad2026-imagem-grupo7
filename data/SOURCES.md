# 🔗 Fontes de dados (datasets)

Datasets públicos usados no projeto. Taxonomia comum do projeto:
`healthy · sigatoka · cordana · pestalotiopsis · fusarium_wilt`

## 1. BananaLSD (Bangladesh)
- **Classes:** healthy, sigatoka, cordana, pestalotiopsis
- **Licença:** CC BY 4.0
- **Mendeley:** https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/9tb7k297ff/1
- **Kaggle:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shifatearman/bananalsd
- **Artigo:** https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2352340923006959
